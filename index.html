Privacy Policy

EFFECTIVE 9 JULY 2026 · APPLIES TO THE TAFCAST ANDROID APP

No accounts, no identity. TAFCast has no sign-up, no login, and no user profiles. We do not know who you are.

No personal data collected. TAFCast does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, and no trackers in the app.

Your data stays on your device. Your list of airports, your card order, and all cached weather data (METAR, TAF, wind forecasts, NOTAMs) are stored locally on your device only. Uninstalling the app deletes them.

Network requests. To fetch weather, the app contacts public data sources — NOAA / aviationweather.gov (METAR and TAF), Open-Meteo (wind forecasts), IPMA (automatic-station observations used for advisory TAFs), and FAA / Autorouter (NOTAMs). These requests contain only the airport identifiers or coordinates needed to answer them. Those services may keep standard server logs (such as IP address) under their own privacy policies.

Permissions. The app requests Internet access and network-state access only — no location, no contacts, no storage beyond its own cache.

Children. TAFCast collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes and contact. Updates to this policy will be posted on this page. Questions: tafcast@outlook.com.
